About Me_

Anna Christ was born in Munich in 1986 and moved to Berlin in 2019.

Between 2012 and 2018, she studied screenplay at the University of Television and Film Munich. Her studies took place under the guidance of Professor Michael Gutmann and Professor Doris Dörrie.

During her student years, she was a scholar of the Hans Boeckler Foundation.

In 2014 she won the “WRITERS ROOM CONTEST“ by SKY & Beta Film. While being a film student, Anna already wrote series bibles and screenplays for production companies such as Phantomfilm and CHR Filmproduktion and developed successful shorts together with her co-students.

From 2016 to 2017 Anna studied Documentary at Capilano University in Vancouver, Canada.

Anna then graduated at the HFF Munich in the spring of 2018 with a diploma in Screenplay.

In 2018 Anna Christ and Jonas Pflaumer wrote the fictional podcast “Fremdgaenger” that got released in 2019 at Hoerverlag-Serials by Random House.

These days, Anna is writing screenplays and show bibles for production companies like Oberon Film and Westside. She works as a dialogue polisher and is developing international documentaries.

Anna is interested in storys with authentic and complex characters. Her documentaries often pay attention to music scenes and subcultures.
